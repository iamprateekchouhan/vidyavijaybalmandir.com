About Us

Vidya Vijay Bal Mandir was founded in year 2003 estd. 1954 with personal experience of Dr. R.k. Dogne's (M.L.A., Harda) struggle of what to do with his life and how to survive in this competitive world. So after getting success in his life, he outlined Vidya Vijay so as other kids don't have to struggle the same. Our campus is close, hygienic, eco friendly and safe infrastructure. Meditation, Yoga, Taekwondo and many such activities are performed for proper physical and mental development of your child. Various activities are done to improve reading and communication skills of children. Indian festivals are celebrated for learning our culture and traditions. Counselling sessions are taken at regular intervals for proper future planning. Students have achieved many district and state level competitions and also achieved Govt. awards like Vishwamitra award, Eklavya awards. Students are guided by teachers who possess more than 10 years of experience in education and various fields. Learning is done with motivational, inspirational leaders for facing life challenges positively. Now currently is progressing by Kunal Dogne.

Vision

Excellent education, Nominal fees

To nurture an environment which is same as the school name i.e. "Victory of education and temple for scholars". To foster a democratic environment that encourages lifelong participation in the acquisition of knowledge and is supportive, innovative and demanding of excellence, combining the best of traditions with modernity. To develop well- rounded, independent thinking and confident individuals with leadership and communication skills who are socially, emotionally and spiritually secure.In here we always try to give the best education without suppressing the talent and nature of kid so that he can perform his best to give back to the society."
